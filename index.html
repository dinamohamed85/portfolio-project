about

Hello, I am Dina Mohamed and I am a web developer. I live and work in Munich . I spend most of my day, experimenting with HTML, CSS, JavaScript (and its endless list of frameworks). I enjoy coding and the challenge of learning something new everyday.

I have 6 years of full-time work experience in Egypt . My last work was mainly with Node.Js in the React Framework, JavaScript, BootStrap, html, Css, CI/CD, Firebase . I started my career as .Net developer as well as with SQL Server. Recently I completed my three-month internship job as Software Engineer at Digital Product School by UnternehmerTUM in Munich. Awesome experience to learn new Techs & Tools: Git, Github, Google Cloud, VScode, Slack, Pycharm, Trello, Hangouts Chat. I have good knowledge of (Problem Solving, OOP, Data Structure).
